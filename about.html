The Foreign Observer is a student magazine and society dedicated to analyzing foreign policy initiatives from an inter-disciplinary perspective. Along with maintaining an online magazine, the group will also approach topics such as the NATO mission in Afghanistan, the Ukraine-Russia conflict and other pertinent global issues from economic, foreign policy-focused, national security-based and public health related perspectives. We seek to eventually host events on campus with relevant speakers and present opportunities for student research taking on both journalistic and analytic qualities.
